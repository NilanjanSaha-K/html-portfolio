The Best Movies According to Me

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Avengers: End Game

Humor and heart: Despite its serious stakes, the movie balanced humor and lighthearted moments with emotional weight.

Shershah

The film tells the true story of Captain Vikram Batra, a brave soldier who sacrificed his life for the nation during the Kargil War. This real-life story of courage and patriotism inspired audiences, especially in India.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows

This was the final installment in a beloved series that many readers (and later, moviegoers) had grown up with. It provided closure to the characters' journeys and the overall story arc, offering a sense of satisfaction and emotional resolution.
